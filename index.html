My Favourite Album By Sy Ari Da Kid

A Toxic Heartbreak

It's hard to pick a favourite album. Each one of his albums produces a vibe that no other has provided.

With songs like Separation, Love Too Hard, Pre-Written Vows, this album boasts with great tracks. The Toxicity interlude is perfectly placed to separate the two moods prominent in the album. I dare you to skip a song on this album.
